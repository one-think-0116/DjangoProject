Get involved

  • Do you write about Django? Let us know, and we'll add your feed to this page.
  • Is your site or project powered by Django? Display one of these handy badges to show your support. And add your site to the list of Django-powered sites.
  • If you'd like to contribute to Django, join the django-developers mailing list.
  • Follow code and ticket changes by subscribing to the django-updates mailing list.
  • Report bugs and make feature requests in our ticket system.
  • Contribute documentation to the wiki.
  • Feel free to post comments throughout this site, to augment our documentation. We review comments frequently and integrate them into the official docs. View recent comments.

Get help

  • Check our FAQ first. If you have a basic question that's not answered by the FAQ, file a ticket to tell us you think it should be in there.
  • Chat live with other Django users in the #django IRC channel on irc.freenode.net.
  • Ask questions on the django-users mailing list.
